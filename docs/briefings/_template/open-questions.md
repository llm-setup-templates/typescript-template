## Rules

- **Questions with no owner are not real questions.** If no one can
  answer, it belongs in a research task, not a briefing agenda
- **Blocking questions come first.** Re-order the table so blocking
  items are at the top — the event may run out of time, and blocking
  items must be resolved
- **Non-blocking questions may be deferred.** Mark them for the next
  briefing rather than forcing a rushed answer
